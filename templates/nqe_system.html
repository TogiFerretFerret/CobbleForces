The NQE (Not Quite Elo) System v2.0

CobbleForces uses the NQE High-Velocity algorithm, designed for rapid climbing and elite performance recognition.

Standard Elo is zero-sum. NQE is positive-sum and momentum-based, ensuring that elite performers can reach legendary tiers (3000+) quickly without being held back by time penalties or conservative transfer rules.

Phase 1: Seed Calculation

We calculate your Expected Rank (Seed) based on the probability of you beating every other opponent in the contest.

Probability(A beats B) = 1 / (1 + 10^((RatingB - RatingA) / 400))
Seed = 1 + Σ Probability(Opponents beat You)

Phase 2: Performance Rating & "Boss Slayer" Floor

We find the Performance Rating that matches your actual rank. However, NQE v2.0 introduces the Boss Slayer Floor to fix time-penalty issues for hard problems.

Logic: Solving a high-rated problem guarantees a performance floor, regardless of total rank.
  • Solving G (3500) guarantees at least 3300 performance.
  • Solving E (2500) guarantees at least 2300 performance.
Performance = Max(CalculatedPerformance, HardestProblemRating - 200)

Phase 3: High-Velocity Volatility (K)

Instead of a slow drift, NQE v2.0 uses high-velocity multipliers based on your history and performance state.

Dynamic K-Factors:
  • Placement Mode (0-4 contests): K = 0.8 (Massive jumps allowed for new talent).
  • Standard Mode (5+ contests): K = 0.25 (Steady growth).
  • Mercy Mode (Performance < Rating): K = 0.1 (Slow falls to protect against single bad days).

Phase 4: Enlightened Compression

To maintain balance at the absolute peak (3000+), rating gains are slightly compressed to prevent infinite scaling while still allowing "Enlightened" users to reach 4000+.

If Rating > 3000 and Delta > 0: Delta = Delta * 0.8

Phase 5: Idempotency & Fairness

The system tracks every RatedParticipation. If a contest is re-rated (e.g., after a cheating cleanup), your rating is reverted to its state before that contest and recalculated from scratch, ensuring the "Infinite Rating Glitch" is impossible.
