Rating System

CobbleForces uses a custom ELO-based rating system to rank participants based on their performance in contests. Your rating determines your title and the color of your handle across the platform.

How it works

After each contest, the system calculates the "Expected Performance" of every participant against every other participant based on their current ratings.

  • If you perform better than expected (rank higher than people with much higher ratings), your rating increases.
  • If you perform worse than expected, your rating decreases.
  • The magnitude of the change is influenced by the number of participants and the rating difference between you and your opponents.

Note: Ratings are only updated when an Administrator triggers the "Recalculate ELO Ratings" action for a specific contest.
