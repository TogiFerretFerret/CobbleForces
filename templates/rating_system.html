Perks & Icons

Special perks are granted to users who reach Enlightened Tiers. These cosmetic enhancements signify your standing in the community.

  • 👑 Crown Perk: The mark of a Yuri Overlord. Bestows the legendary multi-colored animated handle inspired by the Kessoku Band.
  • 🎖️ Medal Perk: Awarded to Yaoi Masters. Adds a pulsing purple shadow and a medal of distinction to your handle.
  • 🌸 Sakura Trick: Named for obvious reasons, this perk grants Hime-tier users a soft pink pulsing glow reminiscent of cherry blossoms.
  • 🗡️ Protagonist Perk: Standard for all Enlightened users. Signifies that you are the main character of your own competitive programming journey.
